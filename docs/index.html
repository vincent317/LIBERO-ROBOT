LIBERO-Spatial robot learning

ACT policy experiments

A compact record of training and evaluating LeRobot ACT policies for simulated robot manipulation. The main result is an 82% single-task success rate after targeted gripper transition sampling and KL tuning, plus a multitask baseline across all ten spatial tasks.

Results

  • 82% single-task success, 41 / 50 benchmark episodes.
  • 69.4% best verified multitask mean across ten tasks.
  • 62,250 multitask frames converted into a local LeRobot dataset.
  • 10 LIBERO-Spatial tasks handled by one task-conditioned policy.
